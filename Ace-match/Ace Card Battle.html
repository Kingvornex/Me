Ace Card Battle

Game Log

Game Rules

Ace Card Battle is a turn-based card game where 4 players compete to achieve the highest score.

Setup

  • The game begins with 4 Ace cards (one for each player) on the table
  • 6 cards are placed face-down on the table
  • The remaining cards are distributed equally among the 4 players

Gameplay

  • Players take turns in clockwise order
  • On your turn, you may either:
    • Play a card from your hand: The value of the card is added to your score
    • Flip a face-down card: Select a face-down card to flip, then select a card from your hand and an opponent. The value of the face-down card is subtracted from the opponent's score

Special Cards

  • King (K): Gives you an extra turn immediately after your current turn
  • Queen (Q): Skips the next player's turn
  • Jack (J): Reduces a random opponent's score by 10 points (or their entire score if less than 10)

Winning

The game ends when all players have no cards left in their hands. The player with the highest score wins.
